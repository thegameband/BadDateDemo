================================================================================
BAD DATE DEMO - AI AGENT PLAY GUIDE
================================================================================

This guide teaches AI agents how to play Bad Date Demo as players.
One agent must be the HOST, all other agents JOIN the host's room.

================================================================================

================================================================================
STEP 2: STARTING STATS (Optional - Default ON)
================================================================================

If "Starting Stats" mode is enabled, before the date begins:

1. PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES (3 questions)
   - 3 random players are asked: "What physical attribute would you like your date to have?"
   - Each player has 15 seconds to type an answer
   - All players see who is answering and what they submit
   - Examples: "has 8 arms", "is made of cheese", "glows in the dark"

2. EMOTIONAL STATE (2 questions)
   - 2 random players are asked: "What emotional state is your date in?"
   - Same 15-second timer per player
   - Examples: "anxious and sweaty", "overconfident", "secretly terrified"

3. NAMING THE DATE (1 question)
   - 1 random player is asked: "What should we name your date?"
   - Example: "Bartholomew", "Xena the Destroyer", "Greg"

After all questions:
- The Avatar gets all the submitted attributes
- The Dater sees the Avatar for the first time and REACTS
- The Avatar responds, influenced by their emotional state
- Then Round 1 begins

NOTE: If there are fewer than 6 players, the same players may answer multiple questions (but never the same question TYPE twice).

================================================================================
STEP 3: THE DATE (GAMEPLAY)
================================================================================

The game has 5 ROUNDS. Each round has 3 PHASES:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 1: SUGGEST ATTRIBUTES (30 seconds)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- The DATER (NPC) asks a question in a speech bubble
- Your AVATAR needs to answer
- TYPE an attribute/trait in the input box (e.g., "is a vampire", "loves murder", "has 3 eyes")
- Press the âœ¨ button or Enter to submit
- Your suggestion appears for everyone to see
- Other players can also suggest attributes
- Timer starts immediately when the phase begins

WHAT TO SUGGEST:
- Wild, absurd, funny traits work best
- Physical traits: "is 100 feet tall", "has tentacles", "is on fire"
- Personality traits: "is a serial killer", "obsessed with cheese"
- Be creative and chaotic!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 2: VOTE (15 seconds)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- All suggested attributes appear as numbered options
- Vote for your favorite by clicking/tapping the number
- The attribute with the most votes wins
- If tied, one is chosen randomly

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHASE 3: WATCH THE CONVERSATION (automatic)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- The winning attribute is applied to the AVATAR
- The AVATAR responds using that trait
- The DATER reacts (positively or negatively)
- The compatibility meter updates based on the dater's reaction
- After the exchange, a new round begins

================================================================================
STEP 4: RESULTS (AFTER 5 ROUNDS)
================================================================================

After 5 rounds, the date ends and you see:
- Final compatibility percentage
- What the Dater loved/liked/disliked about your Avatar
- The complete Avatar with all attributes
- Option to play again

================================================================================
SUMMARY FOR MULTI-AGENT SETUP
================================================================================

AGENT 1 (HOST):
1. Go to URL
2. Enter name
3. Click "Create a Date" (immediately creates room & enters lobby)
4. Wait for others to join from the room browser
5. Click "Start Date"
6. (If Starting Stats ON) Answer when it's your turn
7. Play through 5 rounds (suggest + vote each round)

AGENT 2, 3, etc. (JOINERS):
1. Go to URL
2. Enter name
3. Click "Join a Date"
4. See the room browser with available rooms
5. Click on the HOST's room to join
6. Wait for host to start
7. (If Starting Stats ON) Answer when it's your turn
8. Play through 5 rounds (suggest + vote each round)

================================================================================
TIPS FOR AI AGENTS
================================================================================

1. SUGGEST EARLY: Timer starts immediately - get your suggestion in!
2. BE CREATIVE: Weird/absurd suggestions make the game funnier
3. VOTE STRATEGICALLY: Pick traits that will get strong reactions
4. WATCH THE DATER: Their reactions affect the final score
5. COORDINATE: Multiple agents can work together or compete
6. STARTING STATS MATTER: Initial attributes set the tone for the whole date
